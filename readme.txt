Login, comment, share using any social account like Google, Facebook, Twitter and more. No WordPress account and user credentials to remember.

== Description ==

miniOrange Social Login allows your visitors to choose any of their favourite social apps to login, comment, share and auto-register with your website or blog. One-click login to your WordPress site using any social app like Google, Facebook, LinkedIn etc. This means, if you are logged in to google or facebook, you can comment on any blog or site by simply clicking a `google icon` - no username or password is explicitly required! Simply enable Social Login and you are done.

All other Social Apps are supported through a simple email to info@miniorange.com.

= Features - =

*	One-click login to your website using any social app.
*	Valid user registrations verified by applications such as Google,Facebook,LinkedIn,Salesforce.
*   Choose where to add the Social Login Plugin: login page, registration page , comment form or anywhere on your site using our social login widget.
*   Options to add social sharing widget on homepage, posts, feeds, content pages, etc.
*	Select your favourite theme for icons.
*	NO SETUP required on Google,Facebook,LinkedIn,Salesforce.
*	Automatic user registration after login if the user is not already registered with your site.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =
1. Visit `Plugins > Add New`.
2. Search for `miniOrange - Social Login`. Find and Install `miniOrange - Social Login`.
3. Activate the plugin from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =
1. Download miniOrange - Social Login.
2. Unzip and upload the `miniorange-social-login` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Activate miniOrange - Social Login from your Plugins page.

= Once Activated =
1. Go to `miniOrange - Social Login` from side menu and follow the instructions.
2. Go to `Appearance->Widgets` ,in available widgets you will find `miniOrange - Social Login` widget, drag it to chosen widget area where you want it to appear.
3. Now visit your site and you will see login with widget.
